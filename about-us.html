About Isselo

The team behind India's most reliable restaurant POS system

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Isselo was born out of a simple observation: restaurant owners in India were struggling with complicated, expensive POS systems that required constant internet connectivity. This challenge was visible across numerous restaurants throughout the country.

After researching the market and finding no affordable, reliable solutions, our team of software developers and restaurant industry experts came together to create Isselo - an all-in-one restaurant management system designed specifically for the unique challenges of Indian restaurants.
