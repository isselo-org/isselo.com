FAQs

What is the importance of restaurant billing software?

▼

Is the restaurant billing process complex?

▼

How much time does it take to set up a restaurant pos?

▼
With isselo, you can set up your restaurant POS in less than a day. Our team helps you with the entire setup process.

How can I track my Orders in real time?

▼
isselo provides real-time order tracking through its dashboard and mobile app, allowing you to monitor orders as they happen.
