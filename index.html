Everything you need to know about Isselo POS for your restaurant

How does Isselo work offline?

Isselo POS is designed to work 100% offline. All data is stored locally on your device, and if you have multiple devices on the same network, they will sync with each other without needing internet. This means even during internet outages or in areas with poor connectivity, your restaurant operations continue uninterrupted.

What hardware do I need to use Isselo?

Isselo works on standard Android tablets/phones and Windows computers. For a complete setup, we recommend:

  • Windows PC or Android tablet for your main billing counter
  • Android tablets/phones for waitstaff taking orders
  • Thermal printer for receipts and kitchen orders (compatible with most 58mm/80mm thermal printers)
  • Cash drawer (optional, connects to the thermal printer)

Many restaurants start with just a single device and printer, adding more as they grow.

Is there a monthly fee or just the yearly price?

Isselo POS has a simple pricing model: ₹2999 per year for the Base Plan. No hidden fees, no percentage of sales, no monthly charges. Each additional device costs ₹499 per year. We believe in transparent pricing to make budgeting easier for restaurant owners.

How difficult is it to set up Isselo for my restaurant?

Setting up Isselo is designed to be simple. The process typically includes:

  1. Installing the app on your devices
  2. Creating your menu categories and items
  3. Setting up your table layout
  4. Configuring tax rates for GST compliance
  5. Adding staff accounts with appropriate permissions

Most restaurant owners complete setup in 1-2 hours. Our support team can also assist you with the initial setup through screen sharing.

What kind of reports does Isselo generate?

Isselo generates a wide range of reports to help you understand your business performance:

  • Daily/monthly sales summaries
  • Item-wise sales analysis
  • GST reports for tax filing
  • Staff performance tracking
  • Payment method breakdowns
  • Peak hour analysis
  • Table turnover rates

All reports can be exported as PDF or Excel files for your accountant or further analysis.

What payment methods can Isselo accept?

Isselo supports multiple payment methods including:

  • Cash
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • UPI payments
  • Mobile wallets
  • Custom payment methods (you can add your own)

You can track all payment types in your reports to reconcile your accounts easily.

How do I get support if I have issues?

  • Phone support (24x7 in your local language)
  • Email support at support@isselo.com
  • WhatsApp support for quick queries
  • Remote screen sharing for complex issues
  • In-app help documentation

Our support team consists of restaurant industry experts who understand the challenges you face.
