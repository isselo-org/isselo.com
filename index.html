FAQs

What is the importance of restaurant billing software?

▼
A restaurant billing system helps businesses manage everyday operations like billing, inventory consumption, online orders, CRM, etc.

Is the restaurant billing process complex?

▼
No, isselo makes the billing process simple with its intuitive interface and 3-click billing process.

How much time does it take to set up a restaurant pos?

▼
With isselo, you can set up your restaurant POS in less than a day. Our team helps you with the entire setup process.

How can I track my Orders in real time?

▼
isselo provides real-time order tracking through its dashboard and mobile app, allowing you to monitor orders as they happen.

Do I need to keep my internet connection ON every time I create an order?

▼
No, isselo works offline. You can create orders without an internet connection, and they will sync automatically when you're back online.

Can I operate and print my bills in my regional language?

▼
Yes, isselo supports multiple languages including regional languages for both operation and bill printing.
